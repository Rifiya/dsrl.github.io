TANK 254



Disinfectant Chamber from DSRL.

DSRL's TANK 254 is capable of destroying wide range of viruses, bacteria and microorganisms on various objects. This is done by degrading the DNA of the germs with high intensity UVC radiation.Within a span of 30 seconds the deactivation of virus takes place.

The objects placed inside undergoes 360 degree sanitation with the help of a specially designed mount. With the help of wheels placed below it can be easily transferred like a trolley.

This Disinfectant Chamber also has slabs for placing small things like mobile, keys etc.
